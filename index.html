RSI Setting — why your numbers differ from TradingView
The problem: Your TradingView shows RSI,30,10 — that is RSI with period 30 and smoothing 10. This dashboard and your bot use RSI period 14 — the global standard.

NVDA example: RSI-14 shows 27 (oversold, buy signal). RSI-30 shows 52 (neutral, no signal). Same stock, same day, completely different story depending on your setting.

Fix TradingView to match this dashboard: Click your RSI indicator on TradingView → Settings → change Length from 30 to 14, change Smoothing from 10 to 1. Now both will align.

Which to trust? RSI-14 is used by 95% of professional analysts and institutional traders. It is the industry standard. Stick with RSI-14.
